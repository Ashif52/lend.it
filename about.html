Who We Are

LendIt is a product built to solve everyday access problems.

Many useful items remain unused in homes while others need them temporarily.

The platform enables communities to share resources efficiently and securely.

What LendIt Does

A smarter way to access the things you need.

Radius-based Discovery

Find what you need from verified users within a close geographical radius.

Community Lending

Borrow peer-to-peer securely with built-in identity checks and item insurance.

Local Business Auth

Integrated tracking for local rental shops to digitize their inventory easily.

Loyalty Rewards

Earn community points and discounts the more you share and borrow.
